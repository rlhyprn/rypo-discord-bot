Privacy Policy for Rypo Discord Bot

Effective Date: August 6, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Rypo Discord Bot (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Bot on Discord.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following information:

  • Discord User ID and Username
  • Reputation points, leaderboard rankings, and related statistics
  • Server IDs where the Bot is added
  • Voting status from third-party services (e.g., Top.gg)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely for the purposes of:

  • Providing the reputation and leaderboard features
  • Tracking voting rewards
  • Maintaining bot functionality and improving user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • When integrating with Discord’s API and trusted third-party services (e.g., Top.gg) to provide features

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to operate the Bot. You may request data deletion at any time.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee complete security.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is intended for users 13 years or older. If you believe someone under 13 is using the Bot, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.
